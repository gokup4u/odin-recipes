Jollof recipes

This is Nigeria jollof

Jollof rice is one the traditional meals in Nigeria which is made primarily from rice grains. Eating jollof rice will make you come again and again for more bites. Let's get straight away into the recipes for making this nutritious and ever refreshing meal.

Ingredients

To make this wonderful dish, we need:

  • rice
  • pepper
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • stocked meat
  • vegetable oil
  • salts
  • seasonings
  • water
  • and our cooking utensils.

Steps in the preparation of the jollof rice

  1. Wash your rice to make it clean.
  2. Prepare a stew with the grounded pepper with tomatoes, adding assorted and stocked meat to the stew. Steam to cook.
  3. Add water to the already made stew and allow to boil
  4. Add the rice to the boiling water and add salt and seanonings as desired,
  5. Leave to cook and turn with at intervals.
  6. Your jollof rice is ready for serving.
